About

Andrew Sheerin

Product design leader, hands-on strategist, and behavioural thinker. More than twenty years helping organisations understand and navigate ambiguity, identify the most valuable problems to solve and arm teams with the tools and thinking to execute confidently with limited information.

Biography

At heart, my work is about simple, effective communication with people. Every problem is communication - what people are aware of, what they understand, what they believe, and what they're willing to do about it.

Product design is the primary discipline through which this has happened, but the underlying capability is broader than design. I've spent my career at the intersection of research, strategy, facilitation, and execution - helping teams see what matters and agree on what to do next.

“Andrew has an unusual ability to observe messy real-world behaviour, identify the hidden structure underneath it, and then create language and models that allow everyone else to think more clearly about it.”

Before product design, I founded TerrorBull Games and created War on Terror: The Boardgame - a satirical game that became an international cultural phenomenon. That experience in systems thinking, provocation, and play continues to shape how I approach every project.
