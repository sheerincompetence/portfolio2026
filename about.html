About

Andrew Sheerin

Product design leader, strategist, and behavioural thinker. More than twenty years helping organisations navigate ambiguity and identify the most valuable problems to solve.

Biography

At heart, my work is about simple, effective communication with people. Every problem is communication — what people are aware of, what they understand, what they believe, and what they're willing to do about it.

Product design is the primary discipline through which this has happened, but the underlying capability is broader than design. I've spent my career at the intersection of research, strategy, facilitation, and execution — helping teams see what matters and agree on what to do next.

Before product design, I founded TerrorBull Games and created War on Terror: The Boardgame — a satirical game that became an international cultural phenomenon. That experience in systems thinking, provocation, and play continues to shape how I approach every project. Read more about my origins →
