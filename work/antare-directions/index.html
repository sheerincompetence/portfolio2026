Five ways to film the same story

Same content, same artefacts — five editorial directions. Each page is a scroll-driven sequence: beats, reveals, one climax. Pick the one that feels closest to truth; we'll refine from there.

Direction 01

Apple Keynote

Black stage. One idea per screen. Product-hero imagery. The principle lands like "one more thing."

 Direction 02

Stripe Press

Essay on cream paper. Literary pacing. Illustrations as footnotes to argument. Motion barely there.

 Direction 03

Financial Times

Pink rules, column measure, data callouts. Business-of-design seriousness. Tables as evidence.

 Direction 04

Museum exhibition

Dark gallery. Spotlit artefacts. Wall plaques. Cards crowd the vitrine then disperse.

 Direction 05

Guardian / BBC

Photo essay scrollytelling. Full-bleed bands. Sticky chapters. Journalism's moral weight.
